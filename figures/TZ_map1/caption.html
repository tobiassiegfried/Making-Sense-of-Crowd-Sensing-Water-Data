Map of the study area. The Pangani River Basin is highlighted with red shading. The main rivers are shown in blue colors. Except for the Mokomazi and the Luengera, all tributaries merge on the slopes of Mt. Kilimanjaro and Mt. Meru. The Themi River and its tributaries is shown in the magnified inset to the left. The major command areas in the downstream are delineated with different colors (in the direction of flow, left diversion Kichangani (yellow), right diversion Kigongoni (green), left diversion Mungushi (orange), left diversion Mwandeti (pale blue), right diversion Themi ya Simba (purple).
